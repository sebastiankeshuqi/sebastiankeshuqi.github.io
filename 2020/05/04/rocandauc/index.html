Receiver Operator Characteristic(ROC) and AUC
Created 2020-05-04| Updated 2020-05-05|machine learning
Post View:

Reference: StatQuest with Josh Starmer

ROC

Motivation

在调整threshold的时候，不同的threshold会得到不同的confusion matrices，但对比一坨confusion matrices并不好。因此我们考虑使用Receiver Operator Characteristic graphs来总结并可视化所有threshold的结果

Sample

![Screen Shot 2020-05-05 at 20.15.23](/Users/aronqi/Library/Application Support/typora-user-images/Screen Shot 2020-05-05 at 20.15.23.png)

Analysis

总的来说我们希望True Positive Rate越大越好，希望False Positive Rate越小越好，用这样的二维坐标可视化这些threshold就可以很容易筛选出来
